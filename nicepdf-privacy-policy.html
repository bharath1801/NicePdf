Privacy Policy

Nice PDF Reader
Effective date: 2025-09-22

Nice PDF Reader (“the App”) is a simple, offline PDF viewer. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how the App handles your information.

1. What data we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All PDFs you open remain on your device and are not uploaded to any server controlled by us.

2. How the App uses your device permissions

The App may request the following Android permissions solely to provide core functionality:

  • Read/Write external storage — to open and display PDF files you select and to cache files locally for reliable viewing.
  • Access network state — to detect connectivity status when opening files from cloud-backed providers.
  • Download without notification — used only for seamless file handling/caching where supported by the OS. The App does not perform background data collection.

Permissions are used only for the stated purposes. If you deny a permission, related features may not work.

3. On-device processing

All PDF viewing and caching happens locally on your device. Cached copies are stored in the app’s private cache directory to enable stable viewing of files provided via Android content providers (e.g., content:// URIs). You may clear the App’s cache from your system settings at any time.

4. Third‑party libraries

The App uses open‑source libraries such as Expo and react-native-pdf to provide its functionality. These libraries run on‑device and do not send your PDFs to us. Your device or OS vendor (e.g., Google/Play services) may process limited, non‑identifying telemetry as part of the Android platform itself.

5. Advertising & analytics

The App does not include ads, analytics SDKs, or tracking technologies.

6. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

7. International transfers

We do not operate servers for this App and do not transfer your data internationally.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Effective date” above.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or the App’s privacy practices, contact us at: nicellccom@gmail.com.
